FLASHCARD application

About

This application is a self evaluating platform. A registered user is allowed to create, update, remove cards and delete decks from their dashboard. The user during self evaluation is displayed the front of the card and the back is revealed on hovering. Upon reveal the card prompts three buttons i.e., 'Easy', 'Medium' and 'Hard' depending on the difficulty of the card according to the user. The scoring for these button clicks is:
Easy: +5 points
Medium: +3 points
Hard: +1 point
